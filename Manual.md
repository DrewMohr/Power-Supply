5. Short Circuit Detection - "SHORT"/"OK"
6. System Ready - "NOT READY"/"READY"
7. Front Panel Backlight
8. TMPS - Stands for "Temperature Monitoring -and- Regulatory System" - Shows the Temperature of the Heatsink
